About Us

We, WOW NLP, are a group of NLPers researching in Natural Language Processing from University of Wollongong (UOW), to enable computers understanding and using human language effectively. We focus on theory development, novel techniques and effective solutions of NLP in broad domains, such as Education, Law, Healthcare and Finance management. Specifically, our efforts cover the practical framework for question answering, knowledge graph, representation & reasoning, document retrieval, Chatbot (dialogue systems).
